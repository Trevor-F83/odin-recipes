Lasagna


Lasagna is the perfect cold night dish. With a little preperation, this meal can be put together and thrown in the oven for 50 minutes @ 375 degrees F. Cover it with foil for the first 25 minutes, then let it cook uncovered for the final 25 minutes. Also, it is important to let the lasagna rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before you cut into it.

    Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb each of Italian Sausage & Lean Ground Beef
  • 1 Full Onion & 2 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 Can Crushed Tomatoes, 2 Cans Tomato Sauce & Cans Tomato Paste
  • 2 Teaspoons White Sugar
  • Fresh Parsley, Dried Basil Leaves, Salt, Italian Seasoning, Fennel Seeds, & Black Pepper. Season to taste.
  • 1 Package Store Bought Noodles
  • Parmesan, Mozarella, & Ricotta
  • 1 Egg


    How to Make Lasagna Step-By-Step

    Here is a very brief overview of what you can expecct when you make homemade lasagna:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagana rest before serving

    How to Layer Lasagna

    The detailed layering instructions can be found in the recipe below, but this is the order you'll follow:

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan.
