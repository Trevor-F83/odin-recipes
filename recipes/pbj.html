Peanut Butter and Jelly


Peanut butter and jelly sandwiches are a staple for lunch in my house. It is fast, easy and delicious. Plus, the peanut butter isn't terrible for you!!! Win-win!

    Ingredients:

  • 2 Slices Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

    How to Make:

  1. Toast 2 slices of whichever bread you choose. Personally, I prefer a good marbled rye.
  2. With a knife, spread your jelly on one slice.
  3. After wiping the knife off on the other slice of toast, spread your penaut butter.
  4. Join to 2 slices of toast and enjoy!
